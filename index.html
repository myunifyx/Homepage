Innovation

Unifyx is a forward-thinking platform designed to create impactful solutions for everyday challenges. Our mission is to connect communities, simplify processes, and empower individuals through innovation. One of our key branches, SchoolSwap, tackles the issue of buying and reselling school uniforms. By fostering an accessible and affordable platform, SchoolSwap bridges the gap between families in need of uniforms and those looking to sell.
