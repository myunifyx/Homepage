Faq

What is Unifyx, and what services does it offer?

Unifyx is a platform designed to connect people for seamless service exchange. It offers services such as SchoolSwap, which helps students trade or sell school supplies and uniforms, as well as other tools to facilitate community-driven solutions.

How do I subscribe to Unifyx updates?

You can subscribe to Unifyx updates by entering your email address in the subscription form found on the homepage. You'll receive news, updates, helpful tips, and exclusive offers directly to your inbox.

How does the SchoolSwap feature work?

SchoolSwap allows users to buy, sell, or exchange school supplies and uniforms easily. Simply create a listing with details about the item you want to trade, and interested users can connect with you to complete the transaction.

What should I do if I encounter an issue using the platform?

If you encounter any problems, you can visit our Contact Us page or send an email to our support team. We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours.

Is my personal information safe with Unifyx?

Yes, Unifyx is committed to protecting your privacy. We use secure systems to store and manage your data and never share it with third parties without your consent. Additionally, the platform is only accessible to users with verified school email addresses, ensuring a safe and trustworthy environment. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.
